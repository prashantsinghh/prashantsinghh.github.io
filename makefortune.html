Dare To Make Fortune.

You want to start your own venture, small or big, manufacturing, service industry or digital startup, etc and you don't know what it takes to launch a successful startup or how to become a successful entrepreneur then this FREE seminar is for you. Here you will learn.

1. To take charge of your life and see through your dilemma between career and startup clearly, so that you can chose rationally and objectively between the two and Be Your Own Boss.
2. To understand the Do's and Don'ts of entrepreneurship so that you succeed in your entrepreneurial journey.
3. The qualities and skills required for an entrepreneur to succeed so that your startup don't fail.
4. To convert your passion into workable business idea and subsequently into a successful business plan so that their is clarity of vision, goal and purpose.
5. To create sales and marketing plan, digital marketing and lead generation model, and recruit and nurture winning and professional team so that your business plan is executed impeccably.
6. Basic nuisances and scenario of Indian and global funding and the different players and govt initiatives to promote startups so that you don't miss any good opportunity.
7. Basic understanding of legal and financial guidelinesfor startups so that things like company formation, taxation formalities, investment and other term sheets etc are taken care of.
8. You will also learn to create impressive product deckand business and sound and crisp investor pitch.
